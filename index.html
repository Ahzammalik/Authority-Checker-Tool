About AdProfitX

Learn about our mission and how we help businesses grow

We Provide Digital Marketing Insights

AdProfitX was founded in 2018 with a mission to democratize access to website analytics and competitive intelligence. Our tools are designed to help marketers, business owners, and SEO professionals make data-driven decisions.

Our traffic estimation algorithm analyzes hundreds of data points to provide accurate traffic estimates for any website. We combine multiple data sources including search engine data, social signals, backlink profiles, and historical trends.

While no estimation tool can replace actual analytics data, our system provides the most accurate estimates possible without access to private analytics accounts.
